Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical mixing project take?

The duration of a mixing project depends on the complexity of the track and the number of revisions required. On average, a single song can take anywhere from 4 to 8 hours to mix.

What format should I provide my tracks in?

For the best results, please provide your tracks as high-quality WAV files (24-bit, 48kHz or higher). Each instrument or vocal should be on a separate track.

Do you offer remote mixing services?

Yes, I offer remote mixing services. We can communicate via email or video call to discuss your project and revisions.

How many revisions are included in your mixing service?

My standard mixing package includes up to 3 revisions. Additional revisions can be arranged for an extra fee.
